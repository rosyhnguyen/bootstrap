About me

Hi Everyone

My Name is Rosy H Nguyen

Here's a little about me

I have a strong background in Project Management and Product Design. I have worked for 3 years in the field of Interactive Design in Canada, Malaysia, and Vietnam. Additionally, I have proficient knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Boostrap.

I am a mentor and tutor for first-year design students at Seneca College from 2019 to 2020. I have volunteered for many design organizations, such as DesignX, Jaipur Literature Festival, Nuit Blanche.

Personal Statement

My dream career is to become an expert in UX Research and Product Design. My approach is to focus on the process to tackle the problems to reach a result, not the end result itself. I am hardworking, independent, responsible, and conscientious.


I mentor and tutor many first-year design students at college. I want to convey the can-do attitude to everyone I meet because I strongly believe the right attitude will take you far in your life.
